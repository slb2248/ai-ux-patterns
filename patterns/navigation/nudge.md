## What it is

Proactive nudge is an AI interface design pattern where the AI offers helpful suggestions or assistance without being explicitly prompted, appearing as subtle hints, tooltips, or inline suggestions. This UX pattern helps users discover AI capabilities and improve their work by providing contextual, non-intrusive guidance. The AI analyzes user behavior and content to identify opportunities for improvement or assistance, then offers suggestions that users can accept or dismiss. This pattern is essential for onboarding new users and helping them discover features, but must be implemented carefully to avoid being annoying or intrusive. It's particularly valuable in writing tools, productivity applications, and platforms where proactive assistance enhances user outcomes.

## When to use

Perfect for writing tools, productivity applications, and platforms where proactive, non-intrusive suggestions help users discover features and improve their work.
