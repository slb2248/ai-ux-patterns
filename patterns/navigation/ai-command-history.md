> Recent AI commands with quick replay

## Overview

AI command history is an AI interface design pattern that maintains a searchable history of recent AI commands, actions, or interactions, allowing users to quickly replay, modify, or reference past commands. This UX pattern displays recent commands in a timeline or list, showing what was executed, when, and the results. Users can search history, filter by type or date, and replay commands with modifications. The interface shows command previews, results, and allows quick access to frequently used commands. This pattern is essential for power users, repetitive workflows, and applications where reusing or modifying past AI interactions improves efficiency.

## Good for

Ideal for power users, repetitive workflows, and applications where quick access to and replay of past AI commands improves productivity.

## Seen in

- Terminal history
- Command palettes
- AI chat history
- Automation tools
