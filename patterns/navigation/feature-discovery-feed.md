# Feature Discovery Feed

**Category:** [Navigation](https://aiuxplayground.com/patterns/navigation)  
**Interactive demo:** [https://aiuxplayground.com/pattern/feature-discovery-feed](https://aiuxplayground.com/pattern/feature-discovery-feed)

> Personalized feed of AI features to try

## What it is

Feature discovery feed is an AI interface design pattern that provides a personalized feed or stream of AI features, capabilities, or tips that users might find useful, based on their behavior, goals, and usage patterns. This UX pattern analyzes user activity, identifies gaps in feature usage, and suggests relevant AI capabilities they haven't tried yet. The feed shows feature cards with descriptions, use cases, and quick access buttons. Features are ranked by relevance and likelihood of being useful. This pattern is essential for applications with extensive AI functionality where users may not discover all available features on their own. It improves feature adoption and helps users get maximum value from AI capabilities.

## When to use

Perfect for applications with extensive AI features, productivity tools, and platforms where personalized feature discovery improves adoption and user value.
