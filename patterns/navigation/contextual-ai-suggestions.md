> Task-based AI prompts

## Overview

Contextual AI suggestions is an interface design pattern that proactively suggests relevant AI features or actions based on the user's current task, location in the application, or content they're working with. This UX pattern uses context awareness to surface AI capabilities at the right moment, such as suggesting image generation when a user is in a design canvas, or code completion when writing code. The suggestions appear as subtle prompts, buttons, or inline hints that don't interrupt workflow but provide helpful guidance. This pattern is essential for complex applications where AI features might be hidden or unknown to users. It makes AI assistance feel natural and integrated into the workflow, improving both discoverability and user productivity.

## Good for

Ideal for complex applications, design tools, and productivity platforms where contextual AI suggestions improve feature discovery and workflow efficiency.
