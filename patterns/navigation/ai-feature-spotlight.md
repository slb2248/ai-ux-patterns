## Overview

AI feature spotlight is an interface design pattern that draws user attention to AI capabilities when they first encounter them or when new AI features become available. This UX pattern uses visual highlights, animations, or overlay tooltips to introduce users to AI-powered features, explaining what they do and how to use them. The spotlight typically appears contextually when users enter a relevant area or perform an action that could benefit from AI assistance. This pattern is essential for applications with multiple AI features where users might not discover capabilities on their own. It improves feature discoverability and helps users understand the full potential of AI-powered tools, reducing the learning curve and increasing feature adoption.

## Good for

Perfect for applications with multiple AI features, productivity tools, and platforms where users need guidance to discover AI capabilities.
