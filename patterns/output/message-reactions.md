> Quick emoji reactions

## Overview

Message reactions is an AI interface design pattern that enables users to quickly provide lightweight feedback on AI-generated messages using emoji reactions, without requiring a typed response. This UX pattern displays reaction buttons or emoji pickers that allow users to express sentiment, approval, or specific feedback with a single click. Users can react with thumbs up, thumbs down, heart, or other emojis to indicate their satisfaction, disagreement, or appreciation for the AI's response. This pattern reduces friction in providing feedback, making it easy for users to signal whether AI responses are helpful, accurate, or relevant. The reactions can inform the AI about response quality and help improve future interactions. This pattern is essential for conversational AI interfaces where quick, low-effort feedback mechanisms improve user engagement and help train better AI responses.

## Good for

Perfect for conversational AI interfaces, chat applications, and feedback systems where quick emoji reactions provide lightweight user feedback and engagement.
