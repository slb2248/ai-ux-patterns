# Granular Consent

**Category:** Trust  
**Live demo:** [https://aiuxplayground.com/pattern/granular-consent](https://aiuxplayground.com/pattern/granular-consent)  
**Browse category:** [https://aiuxplayground.com/patterns/trust](https://aiuxplayground.com/patterns/trust)

> Per-capability toggles, not bundled grants

**Seen in:** iOS photo picker, macOS privacy panes, Google Workspace add-on scopes, GitHub Apps fine-grained permissions

**When to use:** Essential for AI assistants, enterprise agents, and connector ecosystems where least-privilege permissions reduce risk and let users tailor scope to the specific task without losing the rest of the integration.
