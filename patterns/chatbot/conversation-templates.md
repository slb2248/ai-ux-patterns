# Conversation Templates

**Category:** [Chatbot](https://aiuxplayground.com/patterns/chatbot)

> Save and reuse conversation starters

## Overview

Conversation templates is an AI interface design pattern that enables users to save, organize, and reuse conversation starters or prompt templates. This UX pattern provides a template library where users can store effective prompts, conversation structures, or multi-turn interaction patterns that they use frequently. Users can create templates for specific tasks like code reviews, content creation, or research, then quickly start new conversations using these templates. The AI can also suggest templates based on user goals or past successful conversations. This pattern accelerates workflow by eliminating the need to recreate effective prompts, making AI interactions more efficient and consistent.

## Good for

Ideal for power users, professionals, and teams who have established workflows and want to standardize their AI interactions.
