> Swipe bot responses

## Overview

Regeneration carousel is an AI interface design pattern that allows users to generate multiple variations of an AI response and easily navigate between them using swipe gestures or arrow buttons. This UX pattern recognizes that AI-generated content often benefits from iteration, giving users the ability to explore different phrasings, styles, or approaches without losing previous versions. Users can regenerate responses and then swipe or click through a carousel of alternatives to find the one that best fits their needs. This pattern is especially valuable for creative writing, content generation, and any use case where users want to explore multiple AI-generated options before selecting the best one.

## Good for

Perfect for creative writing tools, content generation platforms, and AI assistants where users benefit from exploring multiple response variations.
