**Category:** [Commerce](https://aiuxplayground.com/patterns/commerce)

> Summarizing feedback

## What it is

Review summary is an AI interface design pattern that automatically analyzes product reviews and generates concise summaries highlighting key pros, cons, and common themes. This UX pattern helps users quickly understand overall customer sentiment without reading through hundreds of individual reviews. The AI extracts common praise points, frequent complaints, and notable features mentioned across reviews, presenting them in an easy-to-scan format. This pattern is essential for e-commerce platforms where review volume can be overwhelming, enabling users to make informed purchase decisions quickly. It transforms unstructured review data into actionable insights that enhance the shopping experience.

## When to use

Perfect for e-commerce platforms, product review sites, and marketplaces where summarizing large volumes of reviews helps users make faster purchase decisions.
