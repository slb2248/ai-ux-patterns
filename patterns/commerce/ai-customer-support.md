> Proactive support suggestions

## What it is

AI-powered customer support is an AI interface design pattern that uses artificial intelligence to proactively identify customer issues, suggest solutions, and provide intelligent support recommendations before customers explicitly ask for help. This UX pattern analyzes user behavior, order history, and common issues to surface relevant help content, suggest solutions, or offer proactive assistance. The AI can predict when users might need help based on their actions and provide contextual support. This pattern is essential for e-commerce platforms and customer service applications where proactive support improves satisfaction and reduces support ticket volume.

## When to use

Perfect for e-commerce platforms, SaaS applications, and customer service tools where proactive AI support improves satisfaction and reduces support costs.
