# Visual Search

**Category:** [Commerce](https://aiuxplayground.com/patterns/commerce)  
**Interactive demo:** [https://aiuxplayground.com/pattern/visual-search](https://aiuxplayground.com/pattern/visual-search)

> Find products or images from an uploaded photo

## What it is

Visual search is an AI interface design pattern that allows users to find products by uploading or taking a photo instead of typing search queries. This UX pattern uses computer vision and image recognition to identify products, styles, colors, or patterns in images and match them to similar items in the catalog. Users can photograph something they see in real life, upload a screenshot, or use an image from social media to find similar products. This pattern is particularly valuable for fashion, home decor, and visual product categories where describing items in words is difficult. It makes product discovery more intuitive and accessible, especially for visual learners and users who struggle with text-based search.

## When to use

Ideal for fashion retailers, home decor platforms, and visual product categories where image-based search is more intuitive than text descriptions.
