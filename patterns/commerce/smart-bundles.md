> Contextual upsell

## What it is

Smart bundles is an AI interface design pattern that suggests complementary products, add-ons, or bundled deals based on what users have in their cart or are viewing. This UX pattern uses AI to analyze product relationships, user behavior, and purchase patterns to recommend relevant bundles that enhance the user's purchase. Instead of generic upsells, smart bundles provide contextually relevant suggestions like "frequently bought together" or "complete your look" recommendations. This pattern is essential for e-commerce platforms where intelligent product bundling increases average order value while providing genuine value to customers by helping them discover complementary items.

## When to use

Ideal for e-commerce platforms, online marketplaces, and retail applications where intelligent product bundling increases sales and customer satisfaction.
