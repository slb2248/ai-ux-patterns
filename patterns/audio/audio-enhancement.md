## What it is

Audio enhancement is an AI interface design pattern that uses artificial intelligence to improve audio quality by reducing noise, enhancing clarity, removing echoes, and balancing audio levels. This UX pattern provides controls for adjusting enhancement strength, previewing results, and applying enhancements in real-time or to recorded audio. The AI analyzes audio characteristics and applies appropriate filters and processing to improve intelligibility and quality. Users can see before/after comparisons and adjust settings to balance enhancement with natural sound. This pattern is essential for recording tools, communication platforms, and content creation where audio quality directly impacts user experience.

## When to use

Ideal for recording tools, communication platforms, podcasting software, and content creation applications where AI-powered audio enhancement improves quality and clarity.
