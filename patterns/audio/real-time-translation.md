> Live translation during voice conversations

## What it is

Real-time translation is an AI interface design pattern that provides live, simultaneous translation of spoken conversations between speakers of different languages. This UX pattern displays translated text in real-time as participants speak, often with visual indicators showing who is speaking and in which language. The interface may show original and translated text side-by-side, or overlay translations on the conversation. This pattern breaks down language barriers in live conversations, making multilingual communication seamless. It's essential for international meetings, customer support, and global collaboration tools where participants speak different languages.

## When to use

Perfect for international communication tools, customer support platforms, and collaboration applications where real-time language translation enables seamless multilingual conversations.
