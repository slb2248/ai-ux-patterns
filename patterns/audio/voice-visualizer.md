> Feedback for voice mode

## What it is

Voice visualizer is an AI interface design pattern that provides real-time visual feedback during voice interactions, showing users when the AI is listening, processing, or speaking. This UX pattern displays animated visualizations, such as waveform animations, pulsing circles, or sound wave graphics, that respond to audio input and output, giving users clear feedback about the voice interaction state. The visualizer helps users understand when to speak, when the AI is processing their input, and when the AI is responding, reducing confusion and improving the conversational flow. This pattern is essential for voice-first interfaces and voice assistants where visual feedback compensates for the lack of traditional UI cues, making voice interactions more intuitive and reliable.

## When to use

Essential for voice assistants, voice-first applications, and hands-free interfaces where visual feedback enhances user understanding of voice interaction states.
