> Remove unwanted objects from images

## Overview

Object removal is an AI interface design pattern that allows users to select and remove unwanted objects, people, or elements from images, with the AI intelligently filling in the background. This UX pattern uses inpainting technology to analyze the surrounding area and generate realistic content that seamlessly replaces removed objects. Users can brush over objects to remove them, and the AI handles complex backgrounds, textures, and lighting to create natural-looking results. This pattern is essential for photo editing, real estate photography, and content creation where removing distractions or unwanted elements improves image quality without obvious editing artifacts.

## Good for

Perfect for photo editing tools, real estate photography, and content creation where removing unwanted elements improves image quality and composition.
