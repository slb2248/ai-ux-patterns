> Automatic background removal/transparency

## Overview

Background removal is an AI interface design pattern that automatically detects and removes image backgrounds, creating transparent or replacement backgrounds with a single click. This UX pattern uses computer vision and machine learning to identify the subject of an image and separate it from the background, producing clean cutouts without manual masking or selection. Users can preview the result, adjust edge refinement, and choose to replace the background with solid colors, gradients, or other images. The AI handles complex edges like hair, fur, or transparent objects that are difficult to mask manually. This pattern is essential for e-commerce, marketing, and design workflows where product images or subjects need clean backgrounds.

## Good for

Ideal for e-commerce platforms, marketing tools, and design applications where automatic background removal accelerates workflow and improves image quality.
