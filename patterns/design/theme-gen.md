## What it is

Theme generation is an AI interface design pattern that creates cohesive color palettes, design tokens, and complete visual themes from simple inputs like mood keywords, brand descriptions, or reference images. This UX pattern uses AI to understand color theory, accessibility requirements, and design system principles to generate harmonious color schemes that work across light and dark modes. The generated themes typically include primary and secondary colors, semantic colors for states (success, error, warning), and proper contrast ratios for accessibility. This pattern accelerates design system creation and brand development by automating the complex process of color selection and theme generation, ensuring visual consistency and professional results.

## When to use

Perfect for design system creation, brand development tools, and theme customization platforms where generating cohesive visual themes accelerates the design process.
