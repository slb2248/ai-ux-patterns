> Applying visual styles

## What it is

Style transfer is an AI interface design pattern that instantly applies a complete visual aesthetic, including colors, typography, spacing, and design tokens, to wireframes or basic layouts. This UX pattern dramatically accelerates the design process by separating structure from styling, allowing designers to first focus on layout and hierarchy, then apply brand styles with a single action. The AI analyzes the wireframe structure and intelligently applies design system rules, color palettes, and typography choices to create a fully styled interface. This pattern is particularly valuable for design systems and brand consistency, enabling rapid iteration while maintaining visual coherence across different layouts and components.

## When to use

Perfect for design system tools, brand consistency applications, and rapid prototyping workflows where separating structure from styling accelerates design iteration.
