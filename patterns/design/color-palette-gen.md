## What it is

Color palette generator is an AI interface design pattern that extracts harmonious color schemes from uploaded images, text descriptions, or brand guidelines, creating ready-to-use color palettes for design projects. This UX pattern uses computer vision and color theory to analyze images and identify the most representative and aesthetically pleasing colors, then generates complementary palettes that maintain visual harmony. Users can upload photos, describe desired moods or styles, or reference existing brand colors, and the AI generates 5-7 color palettes with hex codes, RGB values, and accessibility information. This pattern is essential for designers who need to quickly extract color schemes from inspiration images or create brand-consistent palettes from scratch.

## When to use

Ideal for design tools, branding applications, and creative platforms where extracting or generating color palettes from visual inspiration accelerates the design workflow.
